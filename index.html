Tony Stark is a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist.

OFFICIAL SYNOPSIS

After a life-threatening incident, Tony Stark creates a mechanized suit of armor to save his own life and escape captivity. He continues to refine his technology, fighting foes in a high-tech suit equipped with advanced flight capabilities, repulsor rays, and cutting-edge weaponry. As the founding benefactor of the Avengers, Tony constantly wrestles with his ego, his legacy, and his ultimate desire to put a "suit of armor around the world".
